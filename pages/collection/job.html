約伯
大樹 譯
第一章  
1. Uz 地區有一個人名叫約伯。他沒有過錯，正直；敬畏神、遠離邪惡。
In the land of Uz there lived a man whose name was Job. This man was blameless and upright; he feared God and shunned evil.
2. 他膝下七個兒子，三個女兒。
He had seven sons and three daughters,
3. 他的家產合計七千隻羊，三千匹駱駝，五百對閹公牛，五百匹母驢，和許多僕婢。他是東方最高尚的人。
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
4. 他的兒子們輪流在各自家中擺設宴席，邀請諸兄弟及三位姊妹赴席饗宴。
His sons used to hold feasts in their homes on their birthdays, and they would invite their three sisters to eat and drink with them.
5. 每次宴席結束，約伯即籌措所需，奉神洗罪：清早起身，按眾子女人數逐一做燔祭。約伯想：「恐怕我的子女犯了罪孽，內心雜念冒犯了上帝。」這是約伯的習慣。
When a period of feasting had run its course, Job would make arrangements for them to be purified. Early in the morning he would sacrifice a burnt offering for each of them, thinking, “Perhaps my children have sinned and cursed God in their hearts.” This was Job’s regular custom.
6. 有一天，眾天使朝見上帝，撒但也混跡其中。
One day the angels came to present themselves before the Lord, and Satan also came with them.
7. 耶和華對撒但說：「你從哪裏來？」撒但回答耶和華說：「我遊蕩於尘世間，巡迴折返。」
The Lord said to Satan, “Where have you come from?” Satan answered the Lord, “From roaming throughout the earth, going back and forth on it.”
8. 神對撒但說：「你有注意到我的僕人約伯嗎？塵世中無人像他；無過錯、正直、敬畏神、遠離罪惡。」
Then the Lord said to Satan, “Have you considered my servant Job? There is no one on earth like him; he is blameless and upright, a man who fears God and shuns evil.”
9. 撒但回答說：「約伯敬畏神，沒有原由嗎？
“Does Job fear God for nothing?” Satan replied.
10. 難道你沒有為他、他的家人、及他擁有的一切圍上籬笆嗎？你賜福他的勞作，以至於他的家畜漫山遍野。
“Have you not put a hedge around him and his household and everything he has? You have blessed the work of his hands, so that his flocks and herds are spread throughout the land.
11. 但若你伸手毀他一切所有的，他必當面背棄你。」
But now stretch out your hand and strike everything he has, and he will surely curse you to your face.”
12. 耶和華對撒但說：「看哪，凡他所有的都在你手中；只是不可伸手加害於他。」於是撒但從耶和華面前退出去。
The Lord said to Satan, “Very well, then, everything he has is in your power, but on the man himself do not lay a finger.” Then Satan went out from the presence of the Lord.
13. 有一天，約伯的兒女正在他們長兄的家裏吃飯喝酒，
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
14. 有報信的來見約伯，說：「牛正耕地，母驢在旁邊吃草，
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
15. 示巴人忽然闖來，把牲畜擄去，並用刀殺了僕人；惟有我一人逃脫，來報信給你。」
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
16. 他還說話的時候，又有人來說：「神從天上降下火來，把羊群和僕人都吞滅了；惟有我一人逃脫，來報信給你。」
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
17. 他還說話的時候，又有人來說：「迦勒底人分成三隊忽然闖來，把駱駝擄去，並用刀殺了僕人；惟有我一人逃脫，來報信給你。」
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
18. 他還說話的時候，又有人來說：「你的兒女正在他們長兄的家裏吃飯喝酒，
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
19. 看哪，有狂風從曠野颳來，襲擊房屋的四角，房屋倒塌在年輕人身上，他們就都死了；惟有我一人逃脫，來報信給你。」
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
20. 約伯就起來，撕裂外袍，剃了頭，俯伏在地敬拜，
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
21. 說：「我赤身出於母胎，也必赤身歸回；賞賜的是耶和華，收取的也是耶和華。耶和華的名是應當稱頌的。」
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
22. 在這一切的事上，約伯並沒有犯罪，也不以神為狂妄。
and he owned seven thousand sheep, three thousand camels, five hundred yoke of oxen and five hundred donkeys, and had a large number of servants. He was the greatest man among all the people of the East.
